HAIKU

Haiku is a traditional form of Japanese poetry. These short poems consist of three lines with a 5-7-5 syllable pattern and often include references to nature and a seasonal element.

TANKA

Tanka is a classical form of Japanese poetry consisting of five lines with a 5-7-5-7-7 syllable pattern. This poetic form allows for deeper emotional expression than Haiku.
